Age-Specific Learning Zones

Each area features age-appropriate lessons, worship, and activities, ensuring your child loves coming to church.

Tiny Explorers

Ages 3 - 5

Focus on God is Love through simple stories, songs, and guided play. (Nursery/Pre-K)

Discovery Zone

Ages 6 - 8

Interactive lessons and crafts teaching core Bible truths and basic Christian principles. (Grades 1-3)

Junior Disciples

Ages 9 - 12

Deeper biblical studies, leadership training, and engaging discussions focused on living out faith. (Grades 4-6)

Security and Trust

We are committed to providing a safe, secure, and trusted environment for every child. Our protocols give parents peace of mind so they can focus on worship.

  • Secure Check-In/Out for authorized pickups only.
  • All Volunteers are Screened and Trained.
  • Clear Health Protocols for a sanitary environment.
