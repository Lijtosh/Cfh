What Should I Wear?

Casual and Comfortable! We encourage you to wear what makes you feel best. Come as you are.

Your First Sunday: Step-by-Step

  • Arrival & Welcome

    Our Hospitality Team will meet you right at the door and show you to a convenient Visitor Parking spot. Don't be shy—we're here to help!

  • CFH Kids Check-In

    If you have children (Ages 3-12), we'll guide you to our secure **CFH Kids** area for a fun, faith-filled morning with our screened volunteers.

  • Worship & Message

    Enjoy heartfelt worship and a practical, Bible-based message designed to apply to your everyday life. **Relax**, you won't be singled out!

  • Connect with Pastors

    Please stop by the Welcome Desk after the service. We have a special gift for you and would love to personally thank you for visiting!
