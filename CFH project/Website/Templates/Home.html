Our Core Beliefs

Delivering Hope

At CFH Chapel, we exist to deliver hope by sharing the love of Jesus and walking alongside each other in faith to make a real difference in our communities.

Our Vision

We are a family that loves Christ above all, loves each other deeply, and discovers and uses our God-given gifts for His service.

What We Do

We focus on making disciples, strengthening relationships, and living out our faith in everyday life. Our mission is to help you deepen your relationship with Jesus.

You Belong Here!

Whether you are exploring faith or have been following Jesus for years, you are welcome here. We can't wait to meet you!
