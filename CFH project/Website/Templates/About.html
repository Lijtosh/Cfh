About CFH Chapel

A family that loves Christ, serves one another, and seeks to make a difference.

Our Mission

We exist to Deliver Hope—to share the love of Jesus, walk alongside each other in faith, and make a real difference in our communities.

Our Vision

We are a family that loves Christ above all, loves each other deeply, and discovers and uses our gifts for God's Service.

Our Core Values

Love

We care for one another as Christ cares for us.

Stewardship

We use our gifts and resources wisely for God's glory.

Discipleship

We follow Jesus and help others do the same.

Devotion

We commit to prayer, studying the Word, and worship.

Faith

We trust God for big things and step out boldly.

Community

We grow together, supporting and encouraging each other.

What We Do

Who We Are

CFH Chapel isn't just a Sunday service—it's a place to connect, grow, and serve. We focus on making disciples, strengthening relationships, and living out our faith in everyday life. Here, you'll find a community where you can deepen your relationship with Jesus and find your God-given purpose.

Our Key Areas of Focus

  • Worship & Prayer:Engaging and uplifting services centered on God.
  • Discipleship & Small Groups: Hope Cells (small groups) and mentorship to help you grow in faith.
  • Community Outreach:** Serving others through evangelism and social action.
  • Children & Youth Ministries: Helping the next generation know and love Jesus.
  • Leadership Development: Equipping people to impact their families, church, and the world.

You Belong Here!

If you are looking for a church where you can experience God's love, build meaningful relationships, and grow spiritually, we'd love to have you join us. Whether you're exploring faith or have been following Jesus for years, you are welcome here.
